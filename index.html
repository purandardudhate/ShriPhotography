At Shri Photography & Films, our style of wedding photography is candid and journalistic. We aim to capture the authentic essence of your love story as it naturally unfolds amidst the presence of your beloved family and friends. We believe in preserving genuine moments without intruding or staging scenes. However, during couple portraits or family portraits, we provide gentle guidance to ensure we capture the most flattering and candid shots possible.

At Shri Photography & Films, we’re all about creating wonderful memories on your big day. Here’s a peek at what we do:
Early Start:
We kick things off bright and early so we can catch all the fun moments leading up to your wedding. This way, everyone gets used to us being around, and we capture all the excitement before the main event.
Getting Ready Shots:
We snap pics while the bride puts on her finishing touches of makeup and the groom makes sure he looks just right. It’s all about capturing the beauty, the excitement, and those last-minute nerves before the ceremony begins.
Capturing the Party Vibes:
Weddings are all about having a blast, and we make sure to capture all the fun moments with your guests. Whether it’s laughter, dancing, or just enjoying each other’s company, we’ll capture the joy and energy of your special day.
Ceremony Moments:
We’ll quietly capture the special moments of your ceremony, like when you exchange vows and rings. It’s a time filled with tradition and love, and we’ll make sure to preserve those memories forever.
Reception Fun:
After the ceremony, it’s time to celebrate! We’ll capture all the smiles, hugs, and laughter as you enjoy your reception with friends and family. From the delicious food to the lively dancing, we’ll capture it all. Plus, we add a special touch to our wedding films by including background sound, making your memories even more magical and unforgettable..

When you choose us for your wedding, you’ll have us by your side for the entire celebration—from getting ready to the ceremony, reception, dance, and party! Our coverage is thorough, and there are no restrictions on the time we spend or the number of photos we take. We’ll take care of capturing every special moment creatively, ensuring that your wedding day memories are beautifully preserved.
